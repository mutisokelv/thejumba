How It Works For Construction Companies & Developers

Work with Jumba for a one stop supply of all your construction materials

  • Step 1

    Get Quote

    Request for a quote through info@thejumba.com. State product, preferred brand if any and quantity

  • Step 2

    Get Prices

    Receive our competitive prices

  • Step 3

    Place Order

    Once you place an order, receive all your products in one truck order.

  • Step 4

    Payment Options

    Available payment options are Mpesa Paybill, Pesalink to our bank account, or a Bank RTGS.

  • Step 5

    Delivery

    Receive your goods as per agreed schedule.
