Construction in Africa, simplified

Jumba is a construction tech platform that simplifies B2B purchase and financing of construction material

With about 70% of construction project costs going to materials, project financiers such as banks can route their financing through Jumba for access of materials.
  • Lenders risk on diversion is limited as funds are directed for construction
  • Better pricing and cost efficiencies are achieved from leveraging economies of scale
  • A bill of quantities and material schedule can be uploaded onto Jumba for materials to be delivered as per schedule ensuring project timelines are met.
